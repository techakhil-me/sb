About

The Heart Behind the Practice

Meet Reshma, a passionate yoga instructor with over 10 years of experience in guiding students through transformative journeys. Her approach combines traditional yoga wisdom with modern mindfulness techniques.

With a background in physical therapy and a deep love for holistic wellness, Reshma creates personalized experiences that help you connect with your inner strength and find balance in today's fast-paced world.
